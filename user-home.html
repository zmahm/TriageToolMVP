Welcome to the NHS AI Health Assistant

Get Help For Your Symptoms

This tool is designed to provide fast, reliable and clinically informed health assessments based on NHS guidelines. By analysing your symptoms, it offers tailored recommendations on whether you should self-manage, visit a pharmacy, consult a GP, call NHS 111, or seek urgent care.

How It Works:

  • Enter your symptoms in a simple guided process.
  • The AI will assess your symptoms against NHS clinical data.
  • Receive clear, actionable advice on your next steps.

This tool does not replace professional medical advice. If you experience severe symptoms, seek medical attention immediately.
